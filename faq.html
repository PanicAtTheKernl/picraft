What is UPS-assisted?

It means the server is connected to a small battery (Uninterruptible Power Supply). If the power goes out, the server doesn’t crash instantly — it has time to shut down safely or ride out short outages.

Is it vanilla?

Yes. No kits, no claims, no pay-to-win. Just survival Minecraft.
