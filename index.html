Pi Craft is a nostalgic, pure Minecraft survival server. No kits, no claims, no pay-to-win nonsense. Just you, the blocks, and the community.

We run on a Raspberry Pi 5 with a proper UPS-assisted power setup, ensuring the server stays online even during short power cuts.
