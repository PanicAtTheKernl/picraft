How to Join

READ THIS: You cannot just connect and start playing. This is a whitelist server. Follow the steps below or forever hold your peace.

Application Process

Step 1: Join our Discord server: discord.gg/3hrtppJwzY

Step 2: Read the rules (yes, actually read them, don't just scroll past like terms of service)

Step 3: Submit your application in the appropriate channel. Tell us:

  • Your Minecraft username (the one you'll actually use)
  • Your age (be honest, we can tell if you're 12)
  • Why you want to join ("because Minecraft is fun" is not an answer)
  • Your building style or what you like to do in Minecraft
  • How you found us

Step 4: Wait for approval (we're not robots, this takes time)

Step 5: Get whitelisted and start playing like a civilized human being

Server Rules

The Basics

  • Don't be a dick: This covers about 90% of everything
  • No griefing: Destroy someone's build, get destroyed yourself (banned)
  • No cheating: X-ray vision belongs in Superman comics, not Minecraft
  • English primary: So we can all understand your terrible jokes
  • Age 16+: Mental age, not just physical age

Building Guidelines

  • Build away from spawn unless it's a community project
  • Don't build right next to someone without asking
  • Clean up your mess when mining or building
  • No massive lag machines or redstone contraptions that break the server
  • Use common sense when claiming land

Community Standards

  • Respect: Don't be a toxic waste of oxygen
  • Teamwork: This is a community, not a solo experience
  • Communication: Use chat responsibly, don't spam
  • Conflict resolution: Talk to staff if there's a problem
  • Common courtesy: Replace what you borrow, clean up shared spaces

Break these rules and you'll find yourself removed faster than you can say "it was just a prank bro."

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I just join without applying?

Answer: No. That's literally what "whitelist" means. Are you new to words?

Do you have creative mode?

Answer: This is survival. If you want creative mode, go play single player.

Can my little brother/sister/cousin/random child join?

Answer: If they're 16+ and can follow rules without supervision, sure. If they need babysitting, no.

Is there a cost to play?

Answer: Just the cost of owning Minecraft. We're not here to empty your wallet.

What version do you run?

Answer: Latest stable version. We update when Mojang stops breaking things.

Can I stream/record on the server?

Answer: Ask permission first. Some players value privacy more than your 3 viewers.

Do you have plugins?

Answer: Yes, but nothing that fundamentally changes the game. Quality of life improvements only.

How long does the application process take?

Answer: Depends how busy we are and how good your application is. Usually a few days to a week.

Can I appeal a ban?

Answer: Sure, but if you got banned, you probably deserved it. Appeals go through Discord.

Do you wipe the world?

Answer: Only when absolutely necessary (major version changes, corruption, etc). Your builds are safe.
