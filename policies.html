Server Policies

Privacy Policy

We collect minimal data necessary for server operation:

  • Minecraft username
  • Discord username (if provided)
  • IP addresses for security

Ban Policy

  • First offense: Warning
  • Second offense: 24-hour ban
  • Third offense: Permanent ban
  • Severe violations: Immediate permanent ban

Appeals

Ban appeals can be submitted through Discord after a 7-day waiting period.

Each case will be reviewed by the admin team within 48 hours.

Data Retention

  • Chat logs: 30 days
  • Player data: Duration of activity
  • Ban records: Indefinitely
